My Reading List


The purpose of this list is TBD. It does motivate me to read more, and I would also like to start writing reviews. This list is the beginning. Many of the books in my room are from the reading lists of other engineers I admire (like Susan Fowler and Patrick Collision).

This year my goal is to read - and finish - 52 books, one per week. 16 books in this year, I am noticing that I am forcing myself to finish books that I don't care for and would normally put down. My hope I will get better at choosing books I enjoy and that I will read more 4 and 3.5 star books in 2020 than I did in 2019, by volume alone.
★★ is Not Great. Can't recommend.
★★1/2 Okay, didn't do it for me.
★★★ Good, enjoyable & overall yes recommendation.
★★★1/2 Very Good, strong recommendation.
★★★★ In Love.

Books that I especially loved are in green.
