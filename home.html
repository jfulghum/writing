Welcome.

My name is Johanna Fulghum. One day in January 2020 I was feeling so inspired by Susan Fowler's reading list that I decided to create my own.

These days I work as an backend software engineer in Denver, CO.

I also host a weekly coffee shop meetup called "Deep Learning in Denver", where I try to replicate the incredible programming meetups that taught me so much in New York City from 2017-2020.

Before my stint in NYC, I worked as an economist for almost four years in Washington D.C, and I still feel like one at heart.


Shoutout to the Code Nation Lower East Side high schoolers -
Working with those students both inspired me and taught me how to create this site.

I have million hobbies. A few of them are soccer, rock climbing, languages, and mountain biking.
