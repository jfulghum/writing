Welcome.

My name is Johanna Fulghum, and currently this site is a blatant ripoff of Susan Fowler's, an engineer and writer I really admire.

I was so impressed with her reading list I decided to create my own.

I know I want to write and read more, and I hope being able share it all with you here will motivate me to do just that.

Thank you github pages!

A little about me, I work as an operations engineer at a healthcare start-up in New York City.

I also volunteer with Code Nation to teach html, css, and javascript to underserved high schoolers in the Lower East Side.

Before moving to New York, I worked as an economist for almost four years in Washington D.C.

When I'm not reading, writing, or coding, I love to play soccer, rock climb, and mountain bike.
