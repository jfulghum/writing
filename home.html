Johanna Fulghum

Welcome.

My name is Johanna Fulghum. I'm a writer, economist, and engineer living in Denver, CO.


One day in January 2020 I was feeling so inspired by Susan Fowler's reading list that I decided to create my own.


These days I work as an backend software engineer at a software company based in Denver, CO. I also host a weekly coffee shop meeup called "Deep Learning in Denver", where I try to replicate the community of incredible meetups where I learned to code in New York City from 2017-2020. Before my stint in NYC, I worked as an economist for almost four years in Washington D.C, and I still feel like one at heart.

I volunteer with Code Nation to teach web development to high schoolers in the Lower East Side twice a week.
Working with the kids both inspired me and taught me how to create this site.


I have million hobbies. A few of them are soccer, rock climbing, and mountain biking.
